MNA Energy (Pvt.) Limited

Who We Are

WEstablished on February 14, 2006, MNA Energy (Pvt.) Limited is a leading LPG marketing company in Pakistan. Registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP), our head office is based in Karachi, with a state-of-the-art LPG storage and bottling plant at Port Qasim.

    Safety & Compliance

    MNA Energy (PVT.) LIMITED adheres to strict safety measures and regulatory approvals, including:

  • ✅ Marketing License from OGRA
  • ✅ NOCs from Chief Inspector of Explosives (CIE), Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), and District Coordination Officer (DCO)
  • ✅ Provisional License for LPG Storage & Filling Plant
  • ✅ ISO 9001:2015 Certification for Quality Management Systems

Our Operations

    LPG Storage & Bottling:

  • Current Capacity: 110 M. Tons
  • Future Expansion: Provision for additional 2000 M. Tons

    • Cylinder Filling:

  • Semi-automatic filling scales from KOSAN Crisplant, Denmark
  • Available cylinder sizes: 11.8 kg, 15 kg, 45.8 kg

    • Transportation:

  • A fleet of LPG tankers ensuring timely and safe deliveries

    • Retail Distribution:

  • Extensive network of dealers and distributors across Pakistan

Our Dedication to Excellence

At MNA ENERGY (PVT.) LIMITED, we are deeply committed to safety, environmental stewardship, and ongoing innovation. Our dedication drives us to:

  • ✅Build a sustainable and energy-efficient future through responsible business practices
  • ✅ Deliver premium-quality, dependable LPG solutions to meet the needs of industries and households
  • ✅ Implement environmentally conscious strategies to minimize our carbon footprint across all operations
  • ✅ Educate and empower communities with awareness programs focused on LPG safety and best practices
